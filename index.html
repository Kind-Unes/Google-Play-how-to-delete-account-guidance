How To Delete Your Account

If you would like to delete your account and all associated data, please follow the steps below:

  1. Open the app on your device.
  2. Go to the "Settings" or "Account" section.
  3. Locate the "Delete Account" button as shown in the screenshot below.
  4. Click on the "Delete Account" option.
  5. Confirm your action when prompted.

Screenshot showing the location of the "Delete Account" button.
