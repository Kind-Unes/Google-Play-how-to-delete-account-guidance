How To Delete Your Account

If you’d like to delete your account and all data, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app on your device (ABBAR on Google Play).
  2. Go to the “Settings” or “Account” section.
  3. Locate the “Delete Account” button as shown below.
  4. Tap “Delete Account.”
  5. Confirm when prompted.
